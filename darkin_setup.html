Map Placement Guide

1

Secure Outer Bases (6x4)

Place 4 Blue Human bases in the leftmost column, and 4 Red Darkîn bases in the rightmost column. Outer columns remain completely fixed.

2

Assemble Neutral Landscape

Prepare 16 middle tiles: 8 barren Normal Lands (blank) and 8 Herb Lands marked with colored plant icons.

3

Shuffle & Randomize

Mix all 16 middle tiles and deal them into the empty slots. Hover over tiles to inspect gathering targets.

Tile Legend
Human Base (4 Tiles) - Left Column (Fixed)
Darkîn Base (4 Tiles) - Right Column (Fixed)
Normal Land (8 Tiles) - Blank Gray Plain
🌿 Herb Land (8 Tiles) - Represented by plant icons
Fixed Bases: 4 Blue (Left) / 4 Red (Right)
Normal Plains: 0 / 8
Herb Wilds: 0 / 8

💡 Hover over any tile to view its name, description, and target value
